ARTSY

The Idea

Artsy is a community for artists. With Artsy, artists can share their work with others, view inspiration, and post best practices on there profiles. Users are able to give feedback by commenting on posts and also sell there art.

Artsy was built using Ruby on Rails, HTML5, CSS, Ruby, Postgresql and bootstrap.
